About this site

I'm a part-time web designer, entrepreneur, angel investor and business advisor (or just substitute unemployed for all of the above!). For the time being, I'm a world traveller.

This site is to record my family's journey round the world. Part vanity project. Part record of our experience. And part travel blog. It's main purpose is for family and friends to keep up with our adventure. Who are we?

We're a family of five, embarking on a once in a lifetime adventure. We're going on a journey around the world. To places that none of us have ever been. And we're going to be away for 239 days.

All together. Every single day...often in a single room.

It's not clear whether the greatest challenge will be home-schooling, the travelling or just being together. But it's going to be fun finding out.

+ From business suit to world traveller

I used to have a proper job. Suited. Commuted. Stressed. No doubt I'll need one again. But for now, I've swapped the suit for shorts and flip flops.

I'm going for the full GAP year experience...but without the backpack...and with more children in tow...and nicer hotels.

About the blog

Part of the education process is for our children to chronicle their journey. For them to practice their writing. For their schoolfriends to keep in touch. So they are going to be writing a weekly update on the site. Hopefully I'll be able to include photos that they've taken themselves too.

I'm also going to be writing a blog. So that we can remember the trip. All the little details that time will erase. To record the highs and lows of the journey. I've not written anything other than business documents for over 20 years. Initially, I've no doubt it'll be rubbish. But I hope the practice of writing every week will help. At the very least I have to improve (don't I?).
