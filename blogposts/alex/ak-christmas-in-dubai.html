Christmas in Dubai

In Dubai, I really like it as it’s always sunny, even on Christmas Day!

We wrote our letters to Santa and I told him that we were going to be in Dubai. I was worried that he might not get my letter. The night before Christmas I was very excited as some Elves came to our room to tuck us in. They read us a story and gave us three chocolate Santas.

And on Christmas Day, I was even more excited as Santa got our letters and came to Dubai.

For Christmas, I got Ronan, Peter Quill, Rocket Racoon, Groot and Drax - the Guardians of the Galaxy and Michaelangelo and Leonardo the Ninja Turtles. Also I got a glow in the dark torch and two top trumps.

At our hotel, there’s a waterpark. And at the waterpark, there’s a little area for kids. At the top there is a colossal bucket and every few minutes it tips and water pours out of it onto the people that are standing under it. The water was really cold so I bought a wetsuit. When you walked further, there’s a big pool that leads onto another big pool and there’s a waterfall and a passage next to it that leads onto the rapid river. It’s all calm though there are seven rapids where the water is really rough. You have to sit in a two or a one rubber ring.

I went on the rapids with Gabriel (Benjamin) who was staying with some friends in Dubai. I was really excited to see him and the rest of his family. Max went on the rapids with his Dad.

Today, we went to the desert and saw camels and some Arabian Oryx. We were in a 4x4 car and we went over really big sand dunes. It was fun. It made my tummy feel funny. It was like a slide in the waterpark!

We went to the 124th floor of the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
