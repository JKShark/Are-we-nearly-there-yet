Disney in Tokyo

Yesterday, we went to Disneyland. It was amazing! First, Daddy took us on a ride called Space Mountain. In the beginning, I felt OK but three quarters of the way in, I was crying because it hurt my tummy and it was too dark. Daddy loved it.

Then I went on Star Tours with 3D glasses. You sat in a room and watched a screen. The room begins shaking. It was like being on a real spaceship (but in Japanese). I loved it.

We then went to Big Thunder Mountain, a roller coaster where you sit in a train. At first I felt like I was going to fall out. It was my favourite ride. It was the best thing ever.

There were long queues everywhere but everyone was having fun and wearing silly hats. I had a Stitch hat. Daddy had a Sully hat (from Monsters Inc). Mummy and Madeleine had Minnie mouse ears.

At the end of the day I felt sad that we were leaving Disneyland, but happy because it was a brilliant day. Hooray, before we left, we got to watch the light show on the castle.
