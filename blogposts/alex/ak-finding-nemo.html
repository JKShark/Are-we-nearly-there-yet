Finding Nemo

Hi, I'm Zippy, the Giant Trevally and I live in the ocean around the Great Barrier Reef. I'm about one metre long because I'm not fully grown yet and I need to eat lots!

As usual I went on my hunting session first thing this morning. On the way I passed my best friends from fish school - my best pal George and my beautiful friend Angel, the yellow and blue angel fish. They came too. We searched for miles, but all we could find was hand-shaped coral. There were loads of types; spiky, soft, smooth and all colours. It looked like a rainbow.

Luckily we didn't give up. We kept searching and found loads of blue-ringed octopuses (they are VERY poisonous so we stayed away from them!) We hunted some more and finally came across a giant school of sardines - a colossal swirling silvery-blue ball. I zipped here and there feeding as the sardines tried to swim away. We ate a lot but we were still hungry (although Angel wasn't, so she went home).

We looked around and spotted something in the distance so we swam to investigate. It was a little tuna on its way to have breakfast, just like us. It led us to its family, but they had no food, so we went off together to catch something big that would last us a lifetime - we feasted on squid, with some puffer fish and trigger fish for pudding.

Finally we were full, so we went to school.
