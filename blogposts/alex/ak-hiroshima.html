Hiroshima

We went to a famous city in Japan, called Hiroshima. It is famous because an American dropped a bomb on Hiroshima and destroyed the city. It destroyed 5 square miles.

The bomb has only been used twice. The bomb is called an atomic bomb. It destroyed everything apart from a government building and we can still see that today.

The bomb was so hot that it melted roof tiles and glass bottles. The bomb actually exploded 600 metres in the air.

I really enjoyed learning about Hiroshima.
