Science Experiments

We went to the science museum in Hong Kong. It was free to get in. There were lots of school children inside. It felt like being back in 2M on a school trip! My two favourite exhibits were:

1. Time of Heart Beat
There was a heart in front of you and a switch. The switch had three different times. It had sleeping, eating and running. The light and the sound changed according to the different activities.

I learned that your heart beat is slowest when you are sleeping, faster when you are eating and it is fastest when you are running.

2. Things That Conduct Electricity
There was a circuit in front of you with 4 gaps in it. You had to find materials that conduct electricity. If they did conduct, the light would come on.

I learned that feathers and rice do NOT conduct electricity but charcoal, iron, wire and foil do.
