Please Sir, can I have some more?

We're 80 days into our journey. Phileas Fogg is already back home. I'm lean, toned and tanned. Everything is perfect.

That's not quite true. I've been eating my way around Asia. And drinking. And after nearly three months, it's time to stop. It's been fun. I've had some great nights out. But my scientific method of assessing my health and fitness (the mirror) is telling me that the all you can eat Asian buffet is now closed. My still fresh running kit is crying for some use.

So, I am officially on a diet.

It's not very manly to admit that you're on a diet. Though I'm not sure why. It's not very manly to waddle around 20kg overweight. As many of you know, one way or another, I'm obsessed with nutrition. Actually, I'm obsessed with food. I've done Paleo, vegan (yeah, I know, not close bedfellows), Atkins, South Beach, 5:2, fasting and anything else out there. I've done exercise. Had blood tests. Run marathons. Done an Ironman. I've been fit. I've been fat. But I've never really been thin. Maybe I should give that a go?

I love the science of food and exercise. Unfortunately I love the taste of food. I left the UK at about 93kg give or take. I'm now (probably) about 105kg. We haven't encountered too many scales, thank god, so I'm guessing a bit. But the mirror and my belt loop are pretty accurate.

I have 160 days until I get home. More than enough time. We are leaving the grey winter of China and entering our Summer loop in Thailand, Indonesia and Australia. No excuses not to be outside. Not to be exercising. I enjoy it too which is fortunate. I just need a kick up the arse to get started.

Once this goes public, I am committed. We're in this together. I'm not going to bang on about it but this is the line in the sand. And I'm stepping across it with my eyes open, facing away from the buffet.

My approach is going to be very complicated. With lots of foods disallowed. Only certain food combinations. Vegan one day. Fruit only the next. Then Paleo. I'll spend Mondays only hopping. Tuesdays I'll sleep standing up to burn more calories. And then...no. I'm going to keep it simple.

I'm going to eat less. Wish me luck.
