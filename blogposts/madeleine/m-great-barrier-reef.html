Great Barrier Reef Adventures

Hi, I'm Sally the Surgeonfish and I live in the Great Barrier Reef. Today is the best day of the week: Surgeonfish-Saturday the 8th of May which means that it's my birthday. My best fishy friends, Daisy the Damselfish (cute and cobalt blue) and spotty Toby the Triggerfish are coming to my party! Mum and I drape slippery, slimey seaweed over bits of super-sharp coral and decorate a huge banner that says "Welcome to Sally's Party!".

At two o'clock my friends arrive. We dart through the coral reef maze to the middle. Inside the maze, purple and blue torch coral waves to us, glittering in the sunlight. Bright purple clams close when we swim over them. Slow, sea turtles swim steadily by. We hear fish crunching on the coral, enjoying an afternoon snack. I love the salty sweet smell of the sea.

In the middle of the maze is a huge box of scrumptious algae which Gabby the Gobie gets to first (of course). She is my sworn enemy. She is fat and greedy and she cheats for every game. I don't know why my mum invited her - yuck! We have a dance-off where I get crowned party-queen and dress up in a gorgeous blue party dress and crown. My mum always says all the fish in the coral reef are pretty enough without clothes,but that doesn't stop me dressing up.

It's time to leave when we spot two huge, plain snorkellers lumbering our way. The Great Barrier Reef is the best place ever, but I wish the humans would leave us alone.
