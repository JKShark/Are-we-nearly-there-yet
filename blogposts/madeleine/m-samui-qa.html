Samui Q&A

Mummy set us some questions and we had to use the internet to find the right answers and then type them up.

1. Where is Koh Samui?
Koh Samui is in the gulf of Thailand, off the east coast of the mainland.

2. Which country is Koh Samui in?
Koh Samui is in Thailand.

3. What does the name of the island mean?
The name of the island means Island Samui. Perhaps Samui is an extension of the name of one of the native trees, mui?

4. What is the country's capital city?
The country's capital city is Bangkok.

5. What language is spoken?
The language spoken is Thai.

6. How many people live on Koh Samui?
62,500 people live on Koh Samui.

7. What's the island's main industry?
The island's main industries are tourism and coconut export. Koh Samui exports 2 million coconuts to Bangkok every month.

8. What is the main religion?
The island's main religion is buddhism.

9. What food do people eat?
A proper Thai meal should consist of a soup, a curry dish with condiments, a dip with accompanying fish and vegetables. A spiced salad may replace the curry dish.

10. How is the island connected to the mainland?
You can either fly or get a boat from the mainland to Koh Samui.

11. How many schools are there on the island?
Around 29 schools (I think).
